In this task you are playing the role of a space scientist on an astrological field trip. You have just discovered some curious stones with mysterious powers! The stones do not all look the same, and how they look seems to be important for how they interact with one another.

The stones discovered so far:

  • come in a range of shades: light, medium, dark, to very dark;
  • and a variety of shapes: triangular , square , pentagon , hexagon , etc.

Sometimes the stones go into an active state in which they glow with a strange light . When an active stone is tapped against an inactive stone, it causes the properties of the inactive stone to change.

Any of the mysterious stones can be active or inactive.

Your task is to come up with theories about what determines these changes, and make some predictions about how some novel stones will affect one another.

Your task will have three phases:

  1. First you will perform 6 tests in which you hit an active stone against an inactive stone and see what happens.

  2. Then, you will be asked to write your best guess of how the phenomena works: what causal rule (or rules) determine when, whether and how the Active stones change the Inactive stones? You will be paid $0.50 bonus if you write the correct hidden causal relation.

While making these predictions, the summaries you saw earlier will still be shown at the top of the page.
